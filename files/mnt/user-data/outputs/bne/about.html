Our Mission

Democratizing access to blockchain and Web3 technologies in Latin America by providing free, high-quality education to public schools and underserved communities.

Part of Refaz Brasil

Blockchain na Escola operates under Refaz Brasil's umbrella, sharing values, infrastructure, and institutional backing. As a Refaz program, BnE benefits from UNESCO partnership (SoST) and organizational support that enables scalable educational initiatives across Brazil.

Our History

2022

The Beginning

First experimental blockchain education program launched in Serra, ES. Blockchain na Escola officially becomes a Refaz Brasil program, establishing foundation for scalable Web3 education.

2023

Rapid Expansion

Expanded to 4 Brazilian states. Conducted 19 workshops in Salvador alone. Received recognition from Ethereum Foundation member for pioneering Web3 education efforts.

2024

Major Milestones

Organized BH Crypto Day - first crypto event in Belo Horizonte history. Presented at DevCon Bangkok. Launched BnE.LAB innovation hub for advanced students.

2025

Scaling Impact

Launched EduLatam curriculum (500+ trained in first week). Mobilized 1,200+ registrations for Modular Carnival. Expanded to Chapada Diamantina with first regional Blockchain Club. Planning expansion to 27 Bahia cities.
